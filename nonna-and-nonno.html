With Love, Nonna and Nonno

This is a collection of photo of my italian grandparents. Their name's are Ida and Antonio and they were both born in Abruzzo, Italy in 1940.

Here is a collection of photos of my nonno and nonna together. Photos of them smiling is rare, they are always bickering but I like to think it's they're love language.

This is Nonna Ida

Nonna is always so excited to see her 3 grandchildren. She's definitely asking if we can stay for espresso and how could we say no!

Nonna stole my look! She saw that my sunglasses matched her shirt and walked in the room and said "Hey! Who wants to go shop?!"

This is my nonna in her kitchen. I took this for my photography class where we had to recreate a famous photographer's photo. The photographer did a take on the painting 'American Gothic'. I called this 'Italian Gothic'.

This is Nonno Antonio

This is the last time I was passanger in my nonno's car. He's getting older and now I get to drive him to the bank, doctor's, and the bakery to get fresh bread.

Nonno loves to make us laugh! During dinner he walked out the room and came back with his covid hair in a new style. Very chic!

Nonno is always in the garden checking on his tomatoes. When I took this photo he wanted everyone to know he collects rain water in huge barrels to water his plants. So I'm guess that's the secret to nice tomatoes.
